Details

All you need to know to get started

WHAT: Trim to Win is a fun way to jump start your New Year’s fitness/weight loss goals. Compete with other men and women throughout the Spokane area to transform your body. Participants will get dietary guidance and a logbook to track progress, pre- and post-challenge assessments (weight, inches, Dexa Scan for body fat, workout), access to 9 area CrossFit gyms, and a T-shirt. Participants are expected to complete 3 workouts per week, track eating using a food journal, and complete the pre- and post-challenge assessments. There will be Men’s and Women's divisions with prizes for the top 3 in each category.

WHERE: Participants have access to 9 area CrossFit gyms for the duration of the challenge. See below for locations.

COST: $297 + $49 assessment fee for non-members / $150 + $49 assessment fee for members

What You Get

This is what you will get from participating in the challenge

Cash

Top 3 men and women will win cash prizes. Winners are selected based on changes in post-challenge assessments.

Coaching

Participants have access to 9 area CrossFit gyms for the duration of the challenge. There are a combined total of 42 coaches whose job is to help you meet your weight and fitness goals.

Dietary Guidance

Each participant receives individual nutrition counseling along with a logbook to track food intake.

Assessment

Pre- and post-challenge assessments include weight and inches measurements, body fat testing with the gold standard Dexa Scan, and completing a baseline workout.
